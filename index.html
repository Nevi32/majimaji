About MAJI MAJI

Trusted partner for comprehensive water solutions in East Africa

East Africa's Water Solution Experts

MAJI MAJI has become East Africa's trusted partner for comprehensive water solutions. Based in Thika town, we specialize in providing reliable access to clean water through professional borehole drilling and sustainable pumping systems.

Our expertise spans across hydro-geological surveys, pump testing, installation services, and solar-powered water systems. We understand the unique challenges of East African terrain and climate, delivering solutions that are both effective and environmentally sustainable.
